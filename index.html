Starter SME

Description:

  • Has a basic understanding of SAP Analytics Cloud and its main components.
  • Can create simple data visualizations and dashboards using pre-built templates and datasets.
  • Can perform basic data exploration and filtering.
  • Understands basic data modeling concepts and can create basic calculations and measures.

Experience / Certifications:

  • Has completed a basic certification or training in SAC.
  • Has participated in at least 1-2 SAC projects as a team member.
  • Has less than 1 year of experience in SAC.

SME

Description:

  • Can independently design and develop complex data models and visualizations in SAP Analytics Cloud.
  • Has a deep understanding of data connections and can integrate various data sources.
  • Can create advanced calculations, formulas, and custom hierarchies.
  • Can design interactive and dynamic dashboards with drill-down capabilities.
  • Has knowledge of advanced features such as predictive analytics and planning capabilities.
  • Can implement intermediate data actions to automate data transformations and workflows.

Experience / Certifications:

  • Has at least one advanced certification in SAC.
  • Has successfully led or co-led 2-3 SAC projects.
  • Has between 1-2 years of experience in SAC.

Senior SME

Description:

  • Can lead and manage end-to-end SAP Analytics Cloud projects, including
    requirements gathering, data modeling, visualization development,
    and deployment.
  • Has a comprehensive understanding of data governance and
    security in SAP Analytics Cloud.
  • Can optimize performance and enhance scalability of
    SAP Analytics Cloud solutions.
  • Can provide guidance and mentorship to
    less experienced team members.
  • Can implement advanced data actions to automate complex data manipulations, data blending,
    and data enrichment processes.

Experience / Certifications:

  • Has multiple advanced certifications in SAC and related tools.
  • Has successfully led 4-6 SAC projects, some of which are complex.
  • Has between 2-4 years of experience in SAC.

Master SME

Description:

  • Recognized as a top-level expert in SAP Analytics Cloud, with
    extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of the platform.
  • Has a deep understanding of advanced analytics capabilities.
  • Can design and implement complex planning and forecasting models.
  • Drives innovation and best practices in SAP Analytics Cloud solutions.
  • Contributes to the SAP Analytics Cloud community through
    thought leadership, blogs, and speaking engagements.
  • Can implement sophisticated data actions involving advanced
    statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and
    real-time data integration.

Experience / Certifications:

  • Has multiple advanced certifications in SAC and related tools, including HANA-focused ones.
  • Has successfully led more than 6 complex SAC projects.
  • Has more than 4 years of experience in SAC.

Senior Master SME

Description:

  • Recognized as an industry expert in SAP Analytics Cloud,
    leading strategic and complex projects.
  • Contributes to external knowledge sharing through blogs, webinars, and
    presentations at industry events.
  • Influences the direction of SAP Analytics Cloud at the industry level and
    has a strong network within the SAP Analytics community.
  • Can design and implement innovative strategies for utilizing SAP Analytics Cloud,
    including integration with non-SAP systems, big data solutions,
    or advanced analytics tools.
  • Can implement highly sophisticated data actions involving advanced algorithms,
    machine learning models, and complex data orchestration processes.

Experience / Certifications:

  • Has contributed to internal knowledge sharing through workshops,
    training, or presentations.
  • Has top-tier certifications in SAC, including HANA-focused ones,
    and possibly a SAP Mentor status.
  • Has successfully led more than 10 complex and strategic SAC projects.
  • Has more than 6 years of experience in SAC.
